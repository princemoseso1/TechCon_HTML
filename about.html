Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of tech enthusiasts and has grown into West Africa's leading technology conference. Over the years, it has featured pioneers in software, AI, and innovation.

Our Mission

We aim to connect innovators, engineers, and creatives to build the future. Our mission is to foster collaboration, spotlight emerging technologies, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders.

Past Speakers

Some of our past speakers include:

  • Ada Nduka Oyom – Founder of She Code Africa, tech community builder, and software engineer.

  • Iyin Aboyeji – Co-founder of Andela and Flutterwave, a visionary in African tech entrepreneurship.

  • Funke Opeke – CEO of MainOne, instrumental in expanding internet access in West Africa.
